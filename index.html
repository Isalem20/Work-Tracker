💾 Backup & Restore

Your data is only saved in this browser on this device — it's not backed up anywhere automatically. If you clear your browser data, switch browsers, or get a new phone, everything will be lost unless you back it up here first.

Save the downloaded file somewhere safe (Google Drive, email to yourself, etc). A reminder banner will appear on the Today page if it's been more than 7 days since your last backup.

⚠️ Restoring will overwrite all current data on this device with the backup file's data.
